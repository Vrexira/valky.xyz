Good day dear visitor, I am Valky!

I am a 3D Custom Model and got created in 3DS Max 2020, but my artificer is living in Switzerland.

My world is a planet called Arborea, and I am here because of Project Valkyria - a browser based game the author of my being is working on. If everything works well, you will be able to choose a character and select your favorite clothes. You can then spawn on a virtual world where you will be able to walk and run around, and use animations as a kind of emotes.

If you would like to see and know more about me and Project Valkyria, you can click on the links above to see me with some diffrent clothes and some animations. You can also read through the history of me and my race, the Castanic.

To inspect me in a full 360 degree view, please use the Web Render - it is also VR compatible :>
