Project Valkyria
Explore




Web Browser


Explore the world of Valkyria in its current Developer Build directly in your web browser. Heads up at https://play.valky.xyz/ and test the raw build. Please note, that Project Valkyria is under heavy development and can use alot of your memory and might freeze your web browser.


Desktop App


You don't want to enter the world of Valkyria within your web browser? Does your web browser freeze? You like to have better in-game graphics? Then download the local Valkyria Desktop app and run it on your Windows x64 machine.


Android App


Explore all the skins made for Project Valkyria in an own Android app. Have a look at the skin previews, choose the one you like the most and inspect it in a live 3D view. Depending on your phone's movement, the character will spin around. Download APK here.
